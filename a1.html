Sam Roberts' Assignment 1: Blink!

Here is all the documentation for Blink! I was tasked to create and code a circuit in which at least 3 LEDs could blink independantly.

This is the schematic for the circuit. Each of the pins (P7, P8, P9) emit 5V when actvated. All three LEDs have a votalge drop of 1.8V. In order to limit the current to be around 20 mA, each LED is paired with a 220 Ohm resistor. This is because V = I * R, so (5 - 1.8) = 0.02 * R results in R = 160. Therefore the ideal resistor to reach 20 mA of current is 160 Ohms, but since the kit I used does not have a resisitor of that value, I used the next closest one of a greater value, since that would guarantee that the current at least will not exceed 20 mA.
